# 📊 XYZ Company – Employee Workhours Dashboard (Office Hours Insights) – 2025

This project analyzes the **actual hours employees spend inside the office** using swipe‑in and swipe‑out logs.  
It provides insights into workforce presence, building utilization, project workloads, and compliance with attendance policies.  
The dashboard helps HR, Operations, and Management make data‑driven decisions.

## ⭐ Key Features

- Summary metrics for employees, buildings, and active projects  
- Quarterly average office hours per project  
- Department‑wise contribution to total workhours  
- Project‑wise and year‑wise comparison of office hours  
- Location‑wise average working hours (2025)  
- Ranking of top employees by quarter  
- Multi‑level slicing: Project, Office Building, Department, Year  
- Clean, minimal, professional design for business use  

---

## 🎯 Business Use Cases

- Detect employees with low or inconsistent office presence  
- Identify employees with unusually high office hours (possible overwork)  
- Optimize building occupancy and seating allocation  
- Compare office‑hour patterns across projects and departments  
- Reduce HR manual attendance corrections  
- Support performance evaluation and compliance discussions  
- Improve workforce planning and space management  

---

## 📈 Key Insights from the Dashboard

- IT Support and Data Engineering show the highest average office presence  
- Wayfair, Westpac, and Veelo projects display stronger quarterly engagement  
- Chennai One and DLF are among the highest‑utilized office locations  
- Verified top employees maintain consistent 10+ hours per day  
- Clear shifts in workload trends across Q1–Q4
